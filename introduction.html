BIOGRAPHY

As a Digital Media Design student at Miami Arts Studio 6-12, Delaila Garcia aspires to be a Graphic Designer and a future business owner. Throughout her years at MAS 6-12, she has been industry certified in Adobe Illustrator as well as awarded “Excellence in Technical Production” for three years in a row and “First Place” in Miami-Dade County Fair's photo restoration competition in 2016. Currently, Delaila’s GPA is at a 3.83, while her weighted GPA is 4.53. While attending MAS 6-12, Delaila has found a passion for graphic designing and plans to open an advertising agency. When Delaila thinks of her future, she thinks of attending Savannah College of Art and Design, Perdue, or University of Central Florida. The fields of study that relate to her projects include the study of graphic design, advertising, digital animation, and marketing. No matter the challenge given at hand Delaila strives for the best and is open to learning. She takes criticism as a means to improve.
